Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Browse through the FAQs below to learn more about our offerings and processes.

1. What services do you offer?

We offer a variety of services that include web development, mobile app development, machine learning solutions, business intelligence, and Power BI dashboards. Our team works with clients to tailor these services to their specific needs, ensuring that each project is unique and addresses key business challenges effectively.

2. How long does it take to complete a project?

The duration of a project depends on its complexity and scope. A standard web development project typically takes around 2-4 weeks, while more complex solutions like custom mobile applications or machine learning models can take 2-3 months. We ensure clear communication with clients to establish realistic timelines based on the requirements.

3. What makes your services unique?

Our services stand out due to our dedication to providing custom, scalable solutions designed to fit the exact needs of our clients. We focus on clean, efficient code, modern design trends, and integrating the latest technologies to enhance user experience and performance. Additionally, we emphasize collaboration and transparency throughout the project lifecycle to ensure client satisfaction.

4. Can I see some examples of your work?

Absolutely! We have a portfolio that showcases a wide range of projects we’ve worked on. From custom websites to app development and data-driven business intelligence solutions, we demonstrate our expertise in various industries. You can explore our portfolio section to see the results of our past collaborations and the innovative solutions we've delivered.

5. Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, we provide post-launch support to ensure that your website or application continues to run smoothly. This includes bug fixes, software updates, and technical assistance for any issues that arise. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages that can be customized based on your needs, ensuring long-term success of your project.

6. What technologies do you use for development?

We use a wide array of modern technologies depending on the project's needs. For web development, we typically use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.js. For backend development, we work with Node.js, Python, or PHP, and integrate databases such as MySQL, MongoDB, or PostgreSQL. We also implement machine learning solutions using Python and relevant libraries, while for business intelligence, we specialize in Power BI, Tableau, and other data visualization tools.

7. What is the cost of a project?

The cost of a project varies based on its scope, complexity, and the resources required. We work closely with our clients to define the project requirements and provide a detailed proposal with a transparent pricing structure. Whether you are looking for a basic website or a complex machine learning solution, we ensure that the cost reflects the value and quality we deliver. Contact us for a personalized quote.

8. How do I get started with Lancway?

Getting started with Lancway is simple! You can contact us through the website's contact form, or send us an email detailing your project needs. Once we receive your inquiry, we'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget. We’ll then provide a proposal outlining the next steps to get the project underway.
